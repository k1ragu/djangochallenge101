Food Types

Meat

Meat is the nutrient-dense edible flesh of animals, primarily consisting of muscle, fat, and connective tissue, offering a high-protein food source often enjoyed cooked, cured, or processed.

Vegetables

Vegetables are edible, nutritious plant parts—including roots, stems, leaves, and flowers—packed with essential vitamins, minerals, and fiber, crucial for a healthy diet and vibrant, flavorful cooking.

Drinks

Drinks are consumable liquids, ranging from hydrating water and nutritious juices to caffeinated coffee, stimulating teas, and varied alcoholic beverages, designed to quench thirst.
